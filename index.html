About Me

Hi, I'm Desmond Gitonyi

I'm a passionate photographer with over 10 years of experience. My work focuses on capturing the raw emotions and timeless beauty in everyday moments.

In a world full of color, I find that removing it reveals the true essence of a subject - the texture, form, emotion, and story that might otherwise be overlooked.

My approach combines technical precision with artistic intuition, creating images that are not just photographs, but visual poems that speak to the soul.
